Tab Structure

How the three main experiences are organized.

3-Tab Structure

Add (numpad) | Feed (shared activity) | Insights (charts). Clean separation of concerns.

Strengths

  • Each tab has one job
  • Numpad is default (Tab 1)
  • 3 tabs = minimal cognitive load
  • Standard iOS pattern

Trade-offs

  • Settings/categories need a 4th tab or gear icon
  • Feed has FAB (redundant with Tab 1)

Recommendation: 3-Tab Structure

Three tabs, three jobs, zero ambiguity. Settings (categories, household) lives behind a gear icon in Feed or Insights navigation bar — not a tab. The FAB on the Feed tab is intentional redundancy: users in the Feed who want to quickly add without switching tabs can tap the glass button to open the entry sheet.
